# 🌍 Data Center Redundancy

## What it is
Running systems across multiple data centers or regions for reliability.

---

## Levels

### Single Data Center
- Simple
- Risky

### Multi Data Center (same region)
- Backup within region

### Multi Region
- Global redundancy
- Highest availability

---

## Benefits
- Disaster recovery
- High availability
- Lower latency globally

---

## Challenges
- Data consistency
- Network latency
- Deployment complexity
